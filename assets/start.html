STAT-gram OVERVIEW


Stat-gram is microservice tool for Business and Channel Influencers on Instagram platform that uses AI to empower how decisions are made and create a positive impact on Marketing and Management.

There is a gap on the market for analytics tools for Instagram business and channel influencers. Leads generation, User experience, meaningful KPI's, Time consuming with Integration with management tools. Using AI, That is what we do!


Following is the complete scope of the features that have been in development procces. New features mid come with users and clients requests and thru user experience research.

Stat-gram is a open-source, we believe that colaborative contribution creates awesome products. Please check out our work and leave your feedback and suggetions, we always want to improve our product and users experiences.
