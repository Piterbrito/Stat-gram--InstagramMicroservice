STAT-gram PRODUCT SCOPE


Stat-gram is microservice tool for Business and Channel Influencers on Instagram platform that uses AI to empower how decisions are made and create a positive impact on Marketing and Management.

There is a gap on the market for analytics tools for Instagram business and channel influencers. Leads generation, User experience, meaningful KPI's, Time consuming with integration with management tools. Using AI, that is what we do!

CUTTING EDGE TECHNOLOGY


Stat-gram delivery a cutting edge technology empower by Machine Learning Algorithms. Our product extract profile data information from all your follows plus a addicional layer with the followers of followers.

What we do with all this data, we process it in the back-end, save the output in the AWS database , then we available all the information for you to play around in our dashboard tool.
